About Chef Booking

Chef Booking helps customers connect with dependable private chefs for home events, celebrations, and curated dining sessions.

What we solve

Hiring a chef should not require endless calls or unclear pricing. We keep the process structured and transparent.

For hosts and families

Find chefs based on cuisine focus, budget, and event size. Send a request without sharing your details publicly.

For working chefs

Build a profile, highlight your strengths, and receive structured booking requests from verified users.

For event planners

Use a repeatable workflow for private dinners, corporate gatherings, and celebration catering.

How it works

1. Explore profiles

Review specialties, experience, and price per person.

2. Submit a request

Pick your date, time, and guest count. The chef receives your booking instantly.

3. Track status

Use your dashboard to monitor requests and confirmations in one place.
