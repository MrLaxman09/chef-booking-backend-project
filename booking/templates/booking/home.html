How booking works

Simple steps with clear outputs.

Step 1: Choose a chef profile

Compare specialty, experience, and cost per person before you request.

Step 2: Enter event details

Select date, time, and guest count to create a complete booking request.

Step 3: Chef responds

Step 4: Track everything in one place
